Welcome to Egg Inc, a stupidly simple mobile game where basically the entire endgame just grinding *(read as: waiting)* for those shiny shiny T4L artifacts.

Unless you're content with a T4L metronome as your best leggy arti the best way to reach True Endgame Player™ status is to craft for those artifacts, but we all know the rates for those are abysmal unless you craft like 200 of an artifact.

Fortuantely, your crafting level can help turn those abysmal rates into slightly less abysmal rates, except by the time you reach level 30 for that sweet sweet 10x legendary chance you probably already have most of the T4Ls you need: a true chicken and egg problem.
